Our Shoes

Our shoes are built for maximum comfort and stability. Our breathable mesh, lightweight materials and padded tongue make for a great in-shoe feel. Also, all products that go into the producing our shoes are made right here in America.

About Achilles Heels Corp.

Achilles Heels was founded by one Simpson College student on September 1th, 2016. With backing from major shoe brands such as Nike, and support from his first company The Sports Website, Inc., Achilles Heels grew exponentially in a few days. Achilles Heels is currently looking for smaller shoe brands to acquire, giving them the assets of a large corporation, while maintaining their independence.

After huge success in both store and online, The Shoe Store caught the attention of famous Midwest shoe company Achilles Heels. In only a matter of days, The Shoe Store was acquired by Achilles Heels Corp. Achilles remains committed to the original Shoe Store brand, and will retain the The Shoe Store business.
