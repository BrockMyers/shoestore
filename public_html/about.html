About Our Company

The Shoe Store was created by two students at Simpson College on September 1st, 2016. The goal the company is to provide outstanding customer service and make sure no one goes without a good pair of shoes, all while giving back to the community where The Shoe Store got its start.

We appreciate the city of Indianola and its surrounding areas. That's why 10% of every purchase you make at our store is given to local charities. The charity changes every month, so make sure to check our news page for updated information.
